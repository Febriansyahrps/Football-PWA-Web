Frank Lampard

         Frank James Lampard is an English professional football manager and former player who is the head coach of Premier League club Chelsea. As a player, he is widely considered to be one of Chelsea's greatest ever players, and one of the greatest midfielders of his generation. He began his coaching career with Championship club Derby County in 2018, leading the club to the final of that season's play-offs. He was appointed as Chelsea's head coach a year later.
